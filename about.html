About Lit Travel

Professional travel planning with a personal touch.

Lit Travel helps clients travel with confidence through warm, reliable service, thoughtful planning and personalised support tailored to the way they want to see the world.

Our story

Built around service that is polished, personal and dependable.

Lit Travel was created for travellers who want more than a standard booking experience. We believe great travel planning should feel clear, supportive and genuinely customer-focused from the first enquiry through to your return home.

Our approach combines professional guidance with personalised recommendations, helping each client choose the right holiday package, flights, accommodation and travel experience for their needs. Whether you are travelling as a family, planning a solo journey or booking a special escape, we work with care and consistency every step of the way.

What clients can expect

  • Professional planning with clear, honest travel advice
  • Personalised recommendations tailored to each traveller
  • Reliable support before, during and after the booking process
  • Customer-focused service designed around comfort and convenience
